Plumbing $500

Electrical $400

Home Builders $500,000

Direct gross revenue per home to the builder. Blended midpoint between custom-build contract value ($300K–$500K) and production sale price (NAHB 2024: $665,298 avg new home with land, $428,215 construction cost only). Excludes land cost.

Property Management $2,400

Industry standard 8–12% of monthly rent. Avg annual gross rental income per landlord ~$20,000–$24,000 (Buildium, iPropertyManagement). Year-1 fee per signed unit.

PI Attorneys $25,000
