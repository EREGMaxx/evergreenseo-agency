# Evergreen SEO Agency — Product Context

## What This Is
The marketing website for Evergreen SEO Agency — a local SEO agency that helps home service contractors (HVAC, roofing, plumbing, electrical, residential contractors) rank on page 1 of Google and generate consistent inbound leads.

## Target Users
- **Primary:** Owner-operators of home service businesses (HVAC, roofing, plumbing, electricians, general contractors)
- **Age range:** 35–60
- **Sophistication:** Low-to-medium on digital marketing. They know they need SEO but don't understand how it works. They've been burned by agencies before.
- **Pain:** Slow seasons, inconsistent lead flow, paying for ads that stop working the moment they stop paying
- **Desire:** Predictable inbound leads, phone ringing without paying per click, ranking above competitors in their city

## Brand Tone
- Confident, direct, no fluff
- Sounds like a seasoned contractor who also happens to know SEO — not a suit
- Trustworthy, not salesy
- Uses plain language — no jargon
- Month-to-month, no contracts — this is a differentiator, lead with it

## Packages
- **Starter:** $397/mo — local SEO fundamentals
- **Growth:** $897/mo — full local SEO + content
- **Full Stack:** $1,297/mo — everything + website build

## Design Direction
- Professional but approachable — not corporate, not flashy
- Dark/navy primary with green accent (brand colors)
- Clean, fast, no unnecessary animations
- Mobile-first — these clients are often on their phone
- Should feel like it could belong to a serious B2B agency but without the stuffiness

## Anti-References
- Avoid: agency sites that look like generic SaaS templates (Webflow showcase vibes)
- Avoid: overly animated, particle effects, too many gradients
- Avoid: stock photography of people in offices shaking hands
- Avoid: buzzword-heavy copy ("synergy", "holistic approach", "360-degree strategy")

## Key Conversion Actions
1. Book a free audit (primary CTA)
2. View pricing
3. Contact via email/phone

## Strategic Principles
- Trust is the product — everything on the page should build credibility
- Specificity converts — "rank #1 for 'HVAC repair Dallas'" beats "grow your business online"
- Speed matters — slow site = distrust signal, especially for contractors who judge fast
